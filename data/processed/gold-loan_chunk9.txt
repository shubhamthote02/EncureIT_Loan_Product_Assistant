If you are considering applying for a gold loan, understanding the loan eligibility is essential.
Age Criteria:
Applicants must be 18 years or older.
Ownership:
Only those who own the gold ornaments or jewellery can pledge them as security.
KYC Compliance:
Basic identity and address proof as per banking guidelines must be provided.
Meeting these eligibility criteria ensures a smooth loan approval process.
