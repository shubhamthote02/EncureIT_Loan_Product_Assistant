For Maha Scholar Education Loan, there is no margin required for Premier Institutions listed under List ‘A’. For List ‘B’ & ‘C’ category Institutes, a minimal 5% margin of the loan amount is required. Under Model Education Loan uptoRs. 4 lakhs, there is no margin required. Above 4 lakhs, there is a required of 5% margin for studies in India and 15% for Studies abroad.
