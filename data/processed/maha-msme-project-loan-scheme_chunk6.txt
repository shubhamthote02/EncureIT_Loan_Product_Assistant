To qualify for the
MSME Project Loan Scheme
, applicants must meet the following criteria:
Business Type:
The applicant must operate within the MSME sector, engaging in manufacturing, trading, or services.
Project Viability:
A well-defined project plan demonstrating feasibility and potential for success is essential.
Compliance:
The business should adhere to statutory requirements and possess necessary registrations as mandated for MSMEs.
