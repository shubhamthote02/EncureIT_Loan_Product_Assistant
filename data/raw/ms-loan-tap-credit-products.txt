Bank has entered in to an agreement with
M/s Loan Tap Credit Products Private Limited (LTCPPL)
under Co-Lending Model (CLM) for providing small business loans to MSME and self-employed segments like Kirana stores.
M/s Loan Tap Credit Products Private Limited, founded on 10th May, 2016, is an online integrated lending platform to facilitate digital lending. The company is headquartered in Pune with its registered office address at: Office No. 103, 1st floor, Hermes Waves, Kalyani Nagar, Pune-411006. The LTCPPL’s digital lending platform, encompasses end-to-end loan processing cycle (Origination, Underwriting, Disbursement, Management and Collection) with automation of major processes.
